Introduction

At GlamAI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and your style. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information we collect

To provide a personalized AI stylist experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile details (e.g., whether you are a student or worker).
  • Visual content (“Closet”): Images of clothing items and closets you upload—this is the core of our service.
  • Wardrobe metadata: AI-generated attributes such as category, color, material, formality level, and price.
  • Usage data: Interactions with the app, including favorite items and accepted outfit suggestions.
  • Device information: IP address, device type, and operating system version.

2. How we use your information

  • Identify and organize: Segment closet photos into individual items.
  • Provide AI insights: Extract details like “warmth level,” “formality,” and “seasonality” to help manage your wardrobe.
  • Generate suggestions: Use style preferences and wardrobe data to suggest daily outfits.
  • Improve the AI: Train internal algorithms to better recognize fashion trends and styles (using de-identified data).

3. AI processing and third‑party services

Image analysis: Your uploaded images may be processed by third‑party AI providers (such as Google Gemini/AI Studio) to identify clothing attributes. By using GlamAI, you consent to the processing of your images by these secure third‑party AI engines.

Cloud storage: We use secure cloud providers (e.g., Firebase, AWS) to store your images and wardrobe metadata.

No sale of data: We do not sell your personal images or closet data to third‑party advertisers.

4. Data retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your wardrobe data and images for as long as your account is active.
  • Item deletion: If you delete an item, the associated metadata and image are removed from our active database.
  • Account deletion: If you delete your account, we will purge your personal data from our systems within [e.g., 30 days], except where required by law.

5. Your rights and choices

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in Europe, CCPA in California), you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about your wardrobe.
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate wardrobe details.
  • Deletion: Request the total deletion of your account and images.
  • Opt‑out: Choose not to provide certain profile details, though this may limit the accuracy of AI outfit suggestions.

6. Security

We implement industry‑standard security measures to protect your images, including encryption of data in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s privacy

GlamAI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app and updating the “Effective Date.”
